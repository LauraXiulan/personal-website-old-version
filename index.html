Sinologist: Knowing all about Chinese Language and Culture.

In 2010 I started the Sinology bachelor at the Leiden University. Here I learned all about writing, speaking, understanding and reading the Chinese language. I also learned much about the immensive Chinese culture. During my bachelor I spent one year abroad, in Taiwan, on a scholarship. Here I really experienced what the Chinese culture entailed. After my bachelors I continued with a master East-Asian studies, where I learned somewhat broader about other countries in East and South-East Asia.

Teacher: Teaching Chinese Language and Culture.

After studying Chinese I decided that I would want to be a teacher. So I enrolled for the Educational Master, again at Leiden University. Here I not only learned how to be a great teacher, but also learned more about adolescent identity and pedagogy, as well as about course didactics.
